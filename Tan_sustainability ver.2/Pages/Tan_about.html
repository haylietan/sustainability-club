About Planet Policy

We aspire to help educate our community to become more conscious of their choices and save our planet. Our hope is to make our school and our community as “green” as possible and reduce the carbon footprint we leave behind on our planet. Conservation includes targeting air pollution caused by vehicles, educating consumers, and contacting large companies.
